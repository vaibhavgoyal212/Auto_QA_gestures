
This directory holds evaluation data used in GENEA (Generation and
Evaluation of Non-verbal Behaviour for Embodied Agents) Workshop 2020 
  https://genea-workshop.github.io/2020/
  https://zenodo.org/communities/genea2020/?page=1&size=20

Test_data/	: ground truth (audio speech + mocap (BVH) data)
BVH_evaluation/ : predicted gestures (in BVH) from speech with 7 systems
GENEA2020SubjectiveResultsAnalyses/: subjective evaluation on predicted gestures
